1. Introduction

Drive Status LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Drive Status mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Personal Identification Information:

  • Full legal name
  • Date of birth
  • Driver's license number
  • State of licensure
  • Email address

Device and Usage Information:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data and interaction logs
  • IP address
  • Crash reports and performance data

Payment Information:

  • Subscription and billing is handled entirely by Apple's in-app purchase system. Drive Status LLC does not collect, store, or process your payment card information directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Verify your identity and process background check requests through our licensed data providers
  • Deliver license status monitoring results to you
  • Manage your subscription and account
  • Send important app updates and notifications
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations under the FCRA and applicable state laws

4. Background Check Data

Drive Status uses a licensed third-party background check service provider to retrieve driver's license status information. When you request a license status check:

  • Your personal information is transmitted securely via encrypted API connection
  • Our provider retrieves your license status from applicable state DMV databases
  • Results are returned to you within the app
  • We do not sell or share your background check results with any third party
  • Data is handled in compliance with the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)
  • Date of birth is collected only at the stage required for identity verification

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Licensed background check provider — to perform license status checks on your behalf
  • Apple Inc. — for subscription management and in-app purchase processing
  • Legal compliance — if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business transfer — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Data Retention

  • We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active
  • Background check data is retained only as long as necessary to deliver results
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us
  • Upon account deletion we will remove your personal data within 30 days

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS and secure API connections
  • Secure backend infrastructure with restricted access controls
  • Webhook security to protect data in transit
  • Regular security monitoring and testing

8. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of certain data processing activities
  • Lodge a complaint with your state's data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights contact us at Clarence.Crawley.ventures@gmail.com

9. Children's Privacy

Drive Status is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information we will delete it immediately.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

11. Sensitive Information

Driver's license information and background check data are considered sensitive personal information. We handle this data with the highest level of care and in full compliance with applicable federal and state laws including the FCRA.

12. Third Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage users to review their privacy policies.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
